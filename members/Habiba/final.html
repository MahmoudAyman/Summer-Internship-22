Final Project:

Consept

Pollution is undoubtedly one of the significant international concerns today. Its effects can be seen everywhere in the air, water, and soil. Waste contributes a lot to the overall pollution for example Landfills have the potential to contaminate soil and water, and the transportation process releases CO2 into the air we breathe in. That is why we must find a way to recycle and do less landfill.

Entering the idea of on-the-spot recycling of waste into readily usable material. The machine will use plastic waste and recycle it into filament to be used in 3D printers. The main focus will be to make it affordable and compact enough for it to be available in every city in Egypt.
