Final Project:

Idea #1: Recycling Machine

Concept

Pollution is undoubtedly one of the significant international concerns today. That is why we must find a way to recycle.

Entering the idea of on-the-spot recycling of waste into readily usable material. The machine will use plastic waste and recycle it into filament to be used in 3D printers. The main focus will be to make it affordable and compact enough for it to be available in every city in Egypt.

Material needed

  • Wood for the can crusher
  • Small Piston
  • Linear Actuator
  • Plastic Grinder
  • Arduino
  • Diameter sensor
  • Heated barrel
  • Extruder
  • On/off Switch
