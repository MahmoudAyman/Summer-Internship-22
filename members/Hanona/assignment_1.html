Step 1: What Do You Need to Make It?

- 3 Slices of bread
- 1/2 cup of Milk
- 1 Egg
- Cinnamon
- Vanilla
- Sugar
- Butter

______________

Step 2: How to make it ?

1. First, In a bowl whisk together the milk, eggs, vanilla, and salt
2. Roll a piece of toast on the egg-milk mixture
3. Melt butter in a large pan over medium heat
4. Place in pan, Turn them until all sides are golden
5. On a plate, combine the sugar and cinnamon powder
6. Transfer them to the plate with the cinnamon sugar and roll to coat all sides
7. Serve with fruits and honey or melted chocolate
