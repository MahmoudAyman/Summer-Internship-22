Assignment 1:How to make crispy fried onion for koshary (تقلية الكشري)

:) ورقة و قلم معايا يا ست الكل

Step 1: Prepareing

I usually go with white onions as they tend to fry up really well. I have used other types of onions as well though and I have never had any complaints.

Clean the onions from their shells and make sure you wash the onions very well.

note that: it's prefered to select a big size of onion (more than 8 cm in diameter).

Step 2: Cutting

Start by cutting the onions into very thin rings about 1 mm thick, separating them from each other.

Step 3: Powdering

Combine the flour, the powdered sugar, and the corn starch. Mix well with onion rings with your hands.

Step 4: Fring

The final step is to fry in a fryer or in a pot with enough oil to cover the onion rings. Once ready, pat dry with some paper towels to get rid of the excess oil. This is important so that they don’t feel too saturated with oil, so don’t skip this step.

This step will take from 7 to 10 minutes.
